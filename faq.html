Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q.1  Are your Websites Custom? Do you use templates?
Yes, everything we build is 100% Custom made with unlimited revisions to the design.

NO, NO and NO NEVER! We do not outsource any of our work to any company or companies we also do not partner with any other company. Everything is build in-house by our developers.
Q.3  What about hosting, Do I have to host my website with you?
No at all unless you want to! Unlike other web design companies at our level we do not need you to host the website with us. We do provide hosting for our customers but it does not means you are obligated to host with us.
